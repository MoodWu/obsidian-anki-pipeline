# motivation

/ˌməʊtɪˈveɪʃn/

## Meaning
noun
1. The reason or reasons one has for acting or behaving in a particular way.
   动机；动力
2. The general desire or willingness of someone to do something.
   积极性；干劲

## Example
Her main motivation for studying abroad is to experience a different culture.

## Translation
她出国留学的主要动机是体验不同的文化。

## Cloze
Without clear ____, it's easy to lose focus on long-term goals.

## Note
词根词缀：来自动词 'motivate'（激励） + 名词后缀 '-tion'，表示行为、状态或结果。
同根词：motivate (v.), motive (n./adj.), motivational (adj.)

Word formation: from the verb 'motivate' + the noun suffix '-tion', indicating an action, state, or result.
Related words: motivate (v.), motive (n./adj.), motivational (adj.)
