# addictive

/əˈdɪktɪv/

## Meaning
adjective
Causing or characterized by addiction.
使人上瘾的；使人入迷的

## Example
The game is so addictive that I can't stop playing it.

## Translation
这个游戏太让人上瘾了，我停不下来。

## Cloze
Social media platforms are designed to be ____ to keep users engaged.

## Note
English: Derived from the verb 'addict' + suffix '-ive' (forming adjectives meaning 'tending to' or 'having the nature of'). Related words: addiction (n.), addict (n./v.).
中文：由动词 'addict'（使上瘾）加后缀 '-ive'（构成形容词，表示'倾向于...的'或'具有...性质的'）构成。同根词：addiction（名词，上瘾），addict（名词/动词，上瘾者/使上瘾）。
