# lack

/læk/

## Meaning
verb
To be without or deficient in something.
缺乏；没有

noun
The state of being without or not having enough of something.
缺乏；不足

## Example
The project failed due to a lack of funding.

## Translation
这个项目因缺乏资金而失败。

## Cloze
Her main weakness is her ____ of confidence in public speaking.

## Note
English: The word 'lack' functions as both a verb and a noun. As a verb, it is regular (lack, lacked, lacking). The noun form is uncountable.
中文：单词 'lack' 既可作动词也可作名词。作动词时是规则变化（lack, lacked, lacking）。名词形式不可数。
