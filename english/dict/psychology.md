# psychology

/saɪˈkɒlədʒi/

## Meaning
noun
1. The scientific study of the human mind and its functions, especially those affecting behavior in a given context.
心理学：对人类心理及其功能的科学研究，特别是在特定情境下影响行为的心理功能。
2. The mental characteristics or attitude of a person or group.
心理特征：个人或群体的心理特征或态度。

## Example
She decided to major in psychology to better understand human behavior.

## Translation
她决定主修心理学，以便更好地理解人类行为。

## Cloze
The study of {{c1::psychology}} helps us understand why people think and act the way they do.

## Note
Psychology comes from the Greek words 'psyche' (soul, mind) and 'logia' (study of). It is often confused with psychiatry, which is a medical field focused on diagnosing and treating mental disorders.
