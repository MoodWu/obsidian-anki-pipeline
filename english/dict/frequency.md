# frequency

/ˈfriːkwənsi/

## Meaning
noun
1. The rate at which something occurs or is repeated over a particular period of time or in a given sample.
频率；发生次数
2. (Physics) The number of complete cycles of a periodic process occurring per unit time.
（物理）频率

## Example
The frequency of earthquakes in this region has increased significantly over the past decade.

## Translation
过去十年间，该地区地震的发生频率显著增加了。

## Cloze
The radio station broadcasts on a specific ...... to reach its target audience.

## Note
词根词缀：源自拉丁语 'frequens'，意为 'crowded, frequent'。同根词：frequent (adj./v.), frequently (adv.)。
Etymology: From Latin 'frequens', meaning 'crowded, frequent'. Related words: frequent (adj./v.), frequently (adv.).
