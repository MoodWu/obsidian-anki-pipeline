# dopamine

/ˈdoʊpəmiːn/

## Meaning
noun
A neurotransmitter in the brain that is involved in regulating movement, emotion, motivation, and feelings of pleasure.
一种在大脑中调节运动、情绪、动机和愉悦感的神经递质。

## Example
Dopamine release is associated with feelings of reward and satisfaction.

## Translation
多巴胺的释放与奖励和满足感相关。

## Cloze
Low levels of ____ can contribute to symptoms of depression.

## Note
词根词缀：dopa-（来自dihydroxyphenylalanine，多巴胺的前体）+ -amine（胺类化合物后缀）。
同根词：dopaminergic（多巴胺能的）。
Etymology: dopa- (from dihydroxyphenylalanine, a precursor to dopamine) + -amine (suffix for amine compounds).
Related words: dopaminergic.
