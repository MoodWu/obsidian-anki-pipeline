# hook

/hʊk/

## Meaning
noun
1. A curved piece of metal or other hard material for catching hold of or hanging things on.
   一个用于钩住或悬挂物品的弯曲金属或其他硬质材料部件。
2. A thing designed to catch people's attention.
   一个旨在吸引人们注意力的东西。
3. (in boxing) A short swinging punch delivered with the elbow bent.
   （拳击中）一种弯曲肘部打出的短摆拳。
verb
1. To attach or fasten with a hook.
   用钩子连接或固定。
2. To catch (a fish) with a hook.
   用鱼钩钓（鱼）。
3. To attract and hold the attention of; captivate.
   吸引并保持...的注意力；迷住。

## Example
He carefully baited the hook before casting his fishing line into the water.

## Translation
在将鱼线抛入水中之前，他小心地在鱼钩上放了鱼饵。
