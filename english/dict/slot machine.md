# slot machine

/ˈslɒt məˌʃiːn/

## Meaning
noun
A gambling machine operated by inserting coins into a slot and pulling a handle or pressing a button to activate spinning reels marked with symbols, which pay out according to patterns when they stop.
一种赌博机器，通过将硬币投入投币口并拉动把手或按下按钮来激活带有符号的旋转卷轴，当卷轴停止时根据图案支付奖金。

## Example
He spent the afternoon playing the slot machines at the casino, hoping to hit the jackpot.

## Translation
他整个下午都在赌场玩老虎机，希望能中头奖。

## Cloze
Many casinos have rows of {{c1::slot machines}} with bright lights and exciting sounds to attract players.

## Note
Slot machine 通常指赌场中的老虎机/角子机，是赌博设备。在非赌博语境中，有时也指自动售货机（vending machine），但更常见的是指赌博机器。
